Project Summary

One of the many scripts I've created for our team at Markham Stouffville Hospital. With the amount of coop students and staff coming into the orginization, I created this script as a solution to cut-down on work time and to automate majority of the work that goes into each account. With this script, I have allowed the ability to create AD accounts in rapid fashion, update passwords, automatically look for next available mnemonic.
