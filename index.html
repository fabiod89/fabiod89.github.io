How it works

our service is straightforward and user-friendly

Sign up and set your preferences

Register online or on the app and choose how you want your laundry to be treated. You can also add any special instructions for us.

Book a pickup time and pack your laundry

Choose a convenient time for us to collect your clothes. For your first order, put your dirty laundry in a reusable or recyclable bag, or a pillow case or a garbage bag.

Let us do the laundry magic

Our team of expert launderers will take good care of your clothes and follow your directions or preferences if given.

Receive your clean and folded clothes

When your clothes are ready, one of our drivers will deliver them neatly folded and packaged to your specified location within 24-48 hours.
