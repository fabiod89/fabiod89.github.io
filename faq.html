F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Do you have any extra charges or hidden fees?

    We include pick up and delivery in our pricing. We are transparent and fair in our pricing and make it easy to understand, as we don't like hidden fees either. All charges will be clearly shown to you before we start the services. Please check the pricing section to avoid any surprises.

  • Can I cancel or reschedule without a fee?

    You can cancel up to 2 hours before your pick up time. You can edit your order up to 1 hour before pick up time. (without any cancellation or rescheduling fees) If you cancel when the driver is at your door, there is a $15 cancellation or missed pick-up fee. If you cancel after pick up but before we have started the cleaning process, there is a $40 cancellation fee. You can't cancel after the cleaning process has started and you will have to pay the full charges for the service.

  • Can I use my own detergent for my laundry?

    Yes, you can provide your own detergent for your laundry.

  • Do I need to be present for the pick up and drop off?

    We offer Contactless pick up and drop off. You can tell us where to pick up/drop off your laundry in a secure place. (Porch, concierge, garage, deck box etc.) We will text/email you when we have picked up/dropped off your laundry. This service is for your convenience and Klaundry is not responsible if your laundry goes missing before pick-up or after drop-off.

  • Do you wash rental tablecloths?

    Yes, we do wash tablecloths at the price of $3.50/lb+tax. Tablecloths need a different process and take 3-5 business days to finish.

  • Do you dry items on a hanger?

    Yes, we do offer dry on a hanger service for $1.25 per item. This service is not available for same day orders.

  • Will my clothes be mixed with other people's clothes?

    No, never. That would not be sanitary. We wash each order separately.
